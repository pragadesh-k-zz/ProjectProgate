Soil

Definiton of soil :

Soil is a mixture of organic matter, minerals, gases, liquids, and organisms that together support life. Earth's body of soil, called the pedosphere, has four important functions: as a medium for plant growth. as a means of water storage, supply and purification.

Based on the crop that you are going to cultivate in your land,the requirement of soil type varies


Soil Crops
sandy soil melon,coconut,Cactus
Clay soil paddy,Lettuce, chard, snap beans
Loamy soil wheat, sugarcane, cotton, jute, pulses, and oilseeds. Vegetables also grow well in this soil.
Red soil groundnuts, pulses, millet, cotton and tobacco
Black soil cotton, sugarcane, tobacco, wheat, millets, and oilseeds.

TYPES OF CROPS :

Food crops- wheat, rice, maize, millets, pulses.
Cash crops- sugarcane, tobacco, jute, cotton, oilseeds.
Horticulture crops- Fruits and vegetables.
Plantation crops- tea, coffee, coconut, rubber.
